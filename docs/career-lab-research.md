# Career lab research (what needs modules)

LearnForge adds **hands-on lab tracks** when a career fails the “quiz-only” test.

## When a career gets a multi-lab track

| Criterion | Why it matters |
|-----------|----------------|
| Daily tools (spreadsheet, terminal, chart, code, forms, jobsite math) | Reading ≠ doing |
| Mistakes cost money, safety, license, or patients | Practice before real risk |
| Employers screen on demonstrated skill | Labs map to hireable tasks |

## Modernization review (2026)

Labs should reflect **how the job works today**, not only textbook tasks from five years ago.

## STEM subjects (non-career)

Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Algebra/Math, Aerospace, Earth Science → Subject Simulations Lab.

## Intentionally not full enterprise sims

No live AD forests, cloud tenants, or paid VMs — in-browser labs only, aligned with TestOut/PhET patterns without their cost model.
